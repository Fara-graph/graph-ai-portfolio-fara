About the Course

This 8-week guided course, "Generative AI for Work & Research Productivity," focuses on leveraging no-code AI tools to automate tasks and enhance efficiency. The curriculum is designed to provide hands-on experience with Large Language Models (LLMs) for a variety of applications, from creating websites to performing complex data analysis and automating research workflows.
